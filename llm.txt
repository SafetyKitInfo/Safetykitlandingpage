# SafetySight

> SafetySight is a mobile-first compliance platform that helps small and medium businesses (SMEs) manage first-aid kit expiry, inventory health, and audit readiness across one or more locations. While purpose-built features exist for ECEC and healthcare, SafetySight serves any SME with WHS first-aid kit obligations — including trades, hospitality, retail, construction, childcare, clinics, and offices. Built for the Australian and New Zealand market.

SafetySight replaces manual spreadsheet-based kit audits with mobile QR scanning, automated expiry alerts, and timestamped audit-ready reports. It supports compliance with Australian WHS regulations, Queensland ECEC standards, the National Quality Framework (NQF), and first-aid kit standards (AS/NZS 4003).

- [Trades & Construction](https://safetykitlandingpage-icsxz5x93-safetykits-projects.vercel.app/#who-we-serve): Businesses with mobile or site-based kit obligations
- [Hospitality & Retail](https://safetykitlandingpage-icsxz5x93-safetykits-projects.vercel.app/#who-we-serve): Venues and stores needing simple, consistent kit checks
- [Disability, Aged Care & Allied Health](https://safetykitlandingpage-icsxz5x93-safetykits-projects.vercel.app/#who-we-serve): Providers with ongoing WHS compliance obligations
- [Offices & Corporate Sites](https://safetykitlandingpage-icsxz5x93-safetykits-projects.vercel.app/#who-we-serve): Any workplace required to maintain compliant first-aid kits under the Work Health and Safety Act

## Key Features

- Mobile QR/barcode scanning with guided checklists
- Automated expiry alerts at 30, 60, and 90 days
- Room-by-room kit tracking (infants, toddlers, preschool, outdoor)
- Inspection scheduling with overdue-check alerts
- Timestamped, exportable audit logs
- Multi-site dashboard with drill-down by centre or room
- Task delegation and guided SOPs for consistent execution
- Inventory health monitoring with reorder suggestions

## About

SafetySight is an early-stage SaaS company founded in Australia, focused on reducing first-aid kit compliance risk for SMEs across any industry — from childcare and healthcare to trades, hospitality, retail, and corporate offices. The platform is guidance and record-keeping software — it supports WHS readiness but does not certify legal compliance or replace professional WHS advice.
